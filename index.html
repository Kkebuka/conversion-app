How to Use

  • Dollar Rate: This is the exchange rate of the period
  • Freight($): The total amount in dollar use in shipping the Container including SONCAP charges
  • Clearing Fee(#): The total amount of money paid to custom officers for clearing goods from wharf
  • Container CBM:This is the volume of goods in the container in respect of the size (CUBIC METER)
  • Carton(s) Price: The carton price of a particular item
  • Total Quantity: The total number of goods in a particular carton
  • CBM of Carton(s):This is the totality of the carton's width, length and height.
  • Price(One piece): This is the price of one piece of the product
  • Carton price:This is the carton Price of the inputed item
